TC-01: Check that the room can be booked with valid data

Description:
Verify that a user can successfully book a room by providing valid data.

Preconditions:

Open the page https://automationintesting.online/

Steps:

1. Choose valid check-in date (20/04/2026)
2. Choose valid check-out date (27/04/2026)
3. Click on the "Check Availability" button
4. Select the Single room and click on the "Book now" button
5. Click on the "Reserve Now" button
6. Fill in the booking form with valid data:
Firstname - John
Lastname - Tester
Email - test@gmail.com
Phone - +380931234567
7. Click on the "Reserve Now" button

Expected Result:

Booking is successfully created
Success message is displayed
Booking details appear in the list/calendar

TC-03: Check that the earlier booked dates show as Unavailable

Description:
Verify that dates that were previously booked are displayed as Unavailable and cannot be selected again.

Preconditions:

There is an existing booking for certain dates

Steps:

1. Choose valid check-in date (20/04/2026)
2. Choose valid check-out date (27/04/2026)
3. Click on the "Check Availability" button
4. Select the Single room and click on the "Book now" button
5. Click on the "Reserve Now" button
6. Fill in the booking form with valid data:
Firstname - John
Lastname - Tester
Email - test@gmail.com
Phone - +380931234567
7. Click on the "Reserve Now" button
8. Click on the "Return Home" button
9. Scroll down and select the Single room and click on the "Book now" button
10. Check that these dates are unavailable in calendar

Expected Result:

Booked dates are marked as Unavailable
